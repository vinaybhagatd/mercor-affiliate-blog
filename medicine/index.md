\## ⭐ What Remote Medicine Work Looks Like



Remote medicine roles often include:



\- Telehealth consultations  

\- Patient triage  

\- EMR documentation  

\- Care coordination  

\- Remote diagnostics  

\- Digital therapeutics support  



\*\*Tools used:\*\* Epic, AthenaHealth, Doxy.me, Zoom Health  

\*\*Skills required:\*\* Clinical judgment, communication, digital literacy  

\*\*Salary range:\*\* ₹8L–₹35L  

\*\*Growth path:\*\* Clinician → care lead → telehealth director
