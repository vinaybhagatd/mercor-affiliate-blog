Remote language roles often include:



\- Translation  

\- Localization  

\- Editing  

\- Proofreading  

\- Transcription

\*\*Skills required:\*\* Grammar, cultural awareness, accuracy  

\*\*Salary range:\*\* ₹5L–₹20L  

\*\*Growth path:\*\* Translator → senior linguist → localization lead
